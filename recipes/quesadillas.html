Crispy Ground Beef Quesadillas

Delicious quesadillas filled with melted cheese, ground beef, corn, avocado, black beans, cilantro, and other flavorful toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp Mexican oregano
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp Taco seasoning
  • 1 yellow onion medium, diced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 15 oz can black beans drained
  • 8 oz can corn kernels drained
  • 1 avocado pitted and chopped
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • cooking spray or vegetable oil
  • 8 oz sharp cheddar cheese shredded
  • 8 oz Monterey Jack cheese shredded

Steps

  1. Shred the cheeses. Dice the onion and avocado. Rinse and drain the beans and corn. Set aside.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, break up and brown the ground beef. Season with kosher salt, pepper, oregano, garlic powder and taco seasoning.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, break up and brown the ground beef. Season with kosher salt, pepper, oregano, garlic powder and taco seasoning.
  4. Add the diced onion and saut√© for another 4-5 minutes.
  5. Mix in the tomato paste, water and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Add the black beans, mix well and set aside
  7. Carefully wipe skillet with paper towel. Spray with cooking spray or a little vegetable oil, and over medium heat, add a tortilla.
  8. On one half of the tortilla sprinkle some of the cheese(s), the beef and beans, corn and avocado and top with more cheese.
  9. Using a spatula, carefully fold tortilla over filling and fry on each side until the cheese is melted and filling heated through. Repeat with remaining tortillas.
  10. Serve with guacamole or your favorite salsas.
